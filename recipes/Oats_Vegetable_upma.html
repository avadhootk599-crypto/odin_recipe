Oats Vegetable Upma

A healthy fiber-rich breakfast that keeps you full longer.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Oats
  • 1 Onion
  • Mixed Vegetables
  • Mustard Seeds
  • Curry Leaves

Steps

  1. Dry roast oats.
  2. Cook onion and vegetables.
  3. Add oats and water.
  4. Cook until soft.
  5. Serve hot.
