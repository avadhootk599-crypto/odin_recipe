Moong Dal Khichdi

A light and healthy Indian meal that is easy to digest.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 Cup Rice
  • 1/2 Cup Moong Dal
  • Cumin Seeds
  • Turmeric Powder
  • Salt
  • Ghee

Steps

  1. Wash rice and dal properly.
  2. Heat ghee and add cumin seeds.
  3. Add rice, dal, and spices.
  4. Add water and pressure cook.
  5. Serve warm.
