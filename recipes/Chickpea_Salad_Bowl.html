Chickpea Salad Bowl

A fresh protein-rich salad perfect for a healthy snack or dinner.

Ingredients

  • 1 Cup Boiled Chickpeas
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Lemon Juice

Steps

  1. Add chickpeas into a bowl.
  2. Add chopped vegetables.
  3. Add salt and lemon juice.
  4. Mix properly.
  5. Serve fresh.
