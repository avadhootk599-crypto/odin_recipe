Banana Peanut Butter Smoothie

Description

A quick high-energy drink thats great for breakfast, gym recovery, or busy college mornings.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1glass milk
  • 1 tsp peanut butter
  • 1 tsp honey (optional)
  • Ice cubes

Steps

  1. Add banana, milk, peanut butter, and honey into a blender.
  2. Blend for 30 to 40 seconds.
  3. Add ice cubes and blend again.
  4. Serve chilled.
