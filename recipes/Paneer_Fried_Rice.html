Paneer Fried Rice

Description

A balanced meal with protein, carbs, and vegetables thats easy to cook in one pan.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup cooked rice
  • 100g paneer
  • 1 onion
  • 1 capsicum
  • 1 carrot
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • Salt and pepper
  • Oil or butter

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan.
  2. Add chopped onion and vegetables.
  3. Cook for 3 to 4 minutes.
  4. Add paneer cubes and cook lightly.
  5. Add rice and soy sauce.
  6. Mix everything well and cook for 2 minutes.
  7. Serve hot.
